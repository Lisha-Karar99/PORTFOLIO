Qualitative Analysis on Watermarking for Preventing Fake Identification

The Water Marking algorithm is used to serve security of image, audio and video data. Now days, it has been being used to keep the privacy and resist the intruders and piracy. To prevent fake identification we use blind and invisible Watermarking. By this way we can recognise an authentic person.
